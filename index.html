    Fun History facts

    In this section some fun historical facts are presented

    Origin

  • Invented in taiwan in the 1980s

    The name

  • Boba is actually slang for large in chinese refferring to the size of the pearls

    • the strugle

  • the first boba drinkers had to eat the pearls with a spoon because the wide straws had not invented yet!

    the name

  • The name bubble tea actually comes from the bubbles created when the tea is shaken to mix it, not from the pearls at the bottom!

    How to make your own boba tea

    in this section you will know that how you can make boba tea at your own

    1. heat water and brown sugar until dissolved
    2. add tapioca starch until dough forms
    3. Roll into tiny balls
    4. boil for 20 minutes then let sit for 20 minutes
    5. Soak them in brown sugar syrup to get that GLOW
